Must-Have Travel Tips

  • Always pack a first-aid kit.
  • Make copies of your important documents (passport, ID).
  • Learn a few basic phrases in the local language.
  • Notify your bank of your travel plans to avoid blocked cards.
  • Stay hydrated and wear comfortable shoes.
